## React Right + Rescue (Koh Tao) — 12.600 THB — KTEN
🛟 The Diver Stress & Rescue Course is a specialty course, which together with the React Right Course is also considered as the next level of dive training after the Advanced course. It's aimed at teaching you everything about stress as a means of preventing diving accidents, as well as how to deal with challenging circumstances when they do arise.
This course will be scheduled in 2 days as follows:
Day 1: Start with theory class about stress in diving: causes, prevention detecting and dealing with it. Then it would be time for you to go to the water and participate in a skills circuit session, where you practice entry level, self rescue, and emergency ascents skills. You'll also learn how to assist a stressed or panicked diver and how to rescue an unconscious diver.
Days 2 & 3: Time for you to put into practice all the things you've learned on day 1. During those 4 open water dives, your Instructor will create scenarios where you'll be asked to act according to your previous training. 
After completing the 4 dives, you'll need to take a written exam to complete your course and get certified as *Rescue Diver*.
📆 Please note that while deposits are non-refundable, you can always change your reservation to a different date or another DPM location at no additional charge.
