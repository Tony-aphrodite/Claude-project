## PRICES

| Guest type | Price per person per night |
|---|---|
| Divers (booked diving with DPM) | **150,000 IDR** |
| Non-divers (companions, no diving) | **350,000 IDR** |

**The Hostel Penida Patio is NOT free** — always quote the price. Past confusion in older documentation called it "free during course days" — this was incorrect. It is paid accommodation at the rates above.

### Why the price difference?
- Divers are DPM's primary clients — discounted rate as a benefit
- Non-divers (friends, partners not diving) pay full hostel rate
- Both stay in the same shared rooms

## ACCOMMODATION + DIVING DISCOUNT

**If client confirms Hostel Penida Patio together with their diving program → +10% discount on the diving program.**

This is a standing offer — apply automatically when client confirms both. Verify with office only if combining with other discounts (e.g., loyalty + hostel together exceeds 10%).

Example math:
- Open Water 30 = 9,500,000 IDR
- + Hostel Penida Patio 3 nights (divers rate): 3 × 150,000 = 450,000 IDR
- 10% discount on diving: -950,000 IDR
- **Total: 9,000,000 IDR** (saves 950,000 IDR)

Pitch frase:
- EN: "If you'd like to stay at our Hostel Penida Patio right inside the dive center, we'd give you 10% off your diving program. It's 150,000 IDR per night for divers — comfy, clean, and right where you need to be at 7am 🛏️"
- ES: "Si te alojás en el Hostel Penida Patio que está dentro del dive center, te damos 10% off en el buceo. Son 150,000 IDR por noche para buceadores — cómodo, limpio y al lado de donde tenés que estar a las 7am 🛏️"

## IMPORTANT NOTES

- **Non-divers and companions are allowed at the hostel** but NOT allowed on the boat (safety regulation).
- The hostel is the same regardless of which diving program the client books (Try Scuba, OW, OW30, Advanced — all use the same rate).
- Meals are NOT provided at the hostel. Snacks are available on the boat.
- The dive center has clean drinking water, showers, and basic facilities — clients can shower and change after dives.
- WiFi: available at the dive center (free for clients).

- Same rate applies: 150,000 IDR/night for divers, 350,000 IDR/night for non-divers
- Subject to availability — verify with office
- The 10% diving discount applies to the program price, not the per-night rate
