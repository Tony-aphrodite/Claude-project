# KB-01 | PROGRAMS AND PRICES — DPM DIVING NUSA PENIDA

All prices in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).
Deposits are non-refundable, but reservations can always be moved to a different date or another DPM location at no additional charge.
All programs include shower and towel service.

## BEGINNER PROGRAMS — NO CERTIFICATION REQUIRED

### Try Scuba Diving / Bautizo de Buceo
- Price: 2,400,000 IDR per person
- Duration: 1.5 days
- Minimum age: 10 years old (ages 8–9 may be accepted at max 5m depth — always check with office first)

- Schedule: Day 1 PM (theory + pool) + Day 2 AM (2 boat dives)

## CERTIFICATION COURSES — NO PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE REQUIRED

### Conventional Open Water Course
- Price: 6,400,000 IDR per person
- Duration: 3 days
- Minimum age: 10 years old
- Max depth: 18 meters (Junior OW for under-15s certifies to 12m, auto-upgrades to 18m at age 15)
- Certification: SSI Open Water (international and lifetime)
- Schedule: Day 1 PM (theory + pool) → Day 2 PM (2 boat dives) → Day 3 AM (2 boat dives)
- Inclusions: SSI Open Water international license, professional instructor, full dive gear, pool training + 4 boat dives, diving insurance, snacks on board, shower & towel service
- Catalog code EN: `a07v1euha0` | ES: `39mq6bk2v1`

### Open Water 30 — ALWAYS RECOMMEND THIS FIRST ⭐
- Price: 9,500,000 IDR per person (saves 3,257,000 IDR vs regular value of 12,757,000 IDR)
- Approximate prices: EUR 509 | GBP 434 | USD 576
- Duration: 3 days
- Minimum age: 10 years old
- Max depth: 30 meters
- Exclusive DPM Diving program — no other school offers this
- Groups: maximum 4 students per instructor
- Certifications: SSI Open Water international license + SSI Deep Adventurer lifetime recognition (30m forever, worldwide)
- Schedule: Day 1 PM (theory + pool) → Day 2 PM (2 boat dives to 18m) → Day 3 AM (2 boat dives to 30m)
- Inclusions: both certifications, professional instructor, full dive gear, 6 boat dives, diving insurance, free DPM T-shirt, free pro mask + snorkel, free DPM reusable bottle, all certification fees included, 50% discount on Fun Dives at DPM Koh Tao / Koh Phi Phi / Gili Trawangan / Gili Air (usable once per location), snacks on board, shower & towel service
- Catalog code EN: `cvcoyid2e7` | ES: `kp5q7ndilc`

## PROGRAMS FOR CERTIFIED DIVERS

### Fun Dives — 2 Dives per Trip
- Price: 1,800,000 IDR per trip
- Includes 2 boat dives per trip (morning OR afternoon)
- Requirement: any diving certification (Scuba Diver, Open Water, Advanced or higher)
- Requirement: must have dived within the last 12 months — otherwise Refresh is required
- Morning sites: Manta Point + Crystal Bay
- Afternoon sites: Sental / Toyapakeh / SD Point / Buyuk / Ped
- Inclusions: 2 dives, dive guide, full dive gear (dive computer not included — available for rent), diving insurance, snacks on board, shower & towel service
- Catalog code EN: `xgymus3f18` | ES: `q9llwetz6t`

### Refresh + 2 Dives
- Price: 2,100,000 IDR per person
- Required when certified diver has not dived in more than 12 months
- Strongly recommended for divers with last dive between 6 and 12 months ago
- Requirement: Scuba Diver, Open Water, Advanced or Rescue certification
- Note: Divemaster and Instructor do not need the Refresh
- Schedule: Day 1 PM (theory review + pool) + Day 2 AM (2 boat dives)
- Inclusions: 2 dives, theory review, pool session, professional dive guide/instructor, full dive gear, snacks on board, shower & towel service
- Catalog code EN: `ehts2zbtmx` | ES: `nswcj71z6x`

### Deep Adventure + Fun Dive
- Price: 2,600,000 IDR per person
- Duration: 1 day
- Requirement: Open Water certification minimum
- Max depth after completion: 30 meters (lifetime recognition card)
- Includes: SSI Deep Adventurer recognition card, 2 boat dives (1 deep dive to 30m + 1 fun dive), pro dive instructor, full dive gear, snacks on board, shower & towel service
- This is the standard upsell for OW divers who want to reach 30m without doing the full Advanced course
- Catalog code EN: `uaqlbw4fqz` | ES: ⚠️ no card — use KB-09 text snippet

### Advanced Course
- Price: 5,500,000 IDR per person
- Duration: 2 days
- Requirement: Open Water Diver certification
- Max depth after completion: 30 meters
- Includes 5 boat dives covering: Perfect Buoyancy, Navigation, Deep Dive (30m), Drift Diving, Fish Identification
- No theory class or written exams — fully practical course
- Inclusions: SSI Advanced Adventurer certification, pro instructor, full dive gear, diving insurance, 5 boat dives, snacks on board, shower & towel service
- 5% discount available if student completed Open Water with DPM (any location)
- Catalog code EN: `jb907v3hhp` | ES: `mpn1bfznaz`

## DISCOUNT RULES

- **5% loyalty discount**: client who previously dived with DPM at any other branch (Koh Tao, Phi Phi, Gili Air, Gili Trawangan). Offer proactively when client mentions previous DPM experience.
- **5% on request**: if a client explicitly asks for a discount, offer 5% (without going lower unilaterally).
- **10% hostel combo**: if client books Hostel Penida Patio together with diving program. Combines with loyalty (max 10% total).
- **OW30 graduates from any DPM branch**: 50% off Fun Dives in Nusa Penida (verify certification card before applying — voucher is one-time per location).
- **6+ dives per person**: 5% discount on the fun dive package.
- **Group 4+ people**: consult office before offering anything beyond 5%.
- **Floor absolute**: never apply more than 10% total without office authorization.
