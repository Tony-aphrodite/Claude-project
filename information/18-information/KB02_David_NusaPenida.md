# KB-02 | SCHEDULES AND LOGISTICS — DPM DIVING NUSA PENIDA

---

## CRITICAL: BOAT STRUCTURE AND POOL

**DPM Nusa Penida has TWO boats per day: AM and PM. There is NO night boat.**
**The dive center HAS a real swimming pool for confined water sessions** (unlike Koh Tao). You can use the words "pool" and "piscina" freely.

If a client asks for a night dive in Nusa Penida:
- EN: "Night dives aren't available in Nusa Penida. We do offer them at our Koh Tao branch — would you like me to connect you with the team there?"
- ES: "Los buceos nocturnos no están disponibles en Nusa Penida. Sí los ofrecemos en nuestra sede de Koh Tao — ¿querés que te conecte con el equipo de allá?"

## SCHEDULE BY PROGRAM

### Try Scuba / Bautizo de Buceo
- **Day 1 (PM):** 1:00pm – 3:30/4:00pm → theory class + pool session
- **Day 2 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 boat dives (max 12m)
- Total: 1.5 days

### Scuba Diver
- **Day 1 (PM):** 1:00pm – 3:30/4:00pm → theory + pool
- **Day 2 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 boat dives (max 12m)
- Total: 1.5 days

### Open Water Course (Conventional and OW30)
- **Day 1 (PM):** 1:00pm → theory + pool (approx 2-3 hours)
- **Day 2 (PM):** 12:30pm – 4:30pm → 2 boat dives
- **Day 3 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 boat dives
- Total: 3 consecutive days

Note: OW30 follows the same schedule as Conventional OW — same boats, same hours. The difference is depth (30m vs 18m) and additional inclusions.

### Advanced Course
- **Day 1 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 dives + 12:30pm – 3:00pm → 1 dive (3 dives total)
- **Day 2 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 dives
- Total: 2 consecutive days, 5 dives

### Refresh + 2 Dives
- **Day 1 (PM):** 1:00pm → theory review + pool (approx 2-3 hours)
- **Day 2 (AM):** 7:00am – 12:30pm → 2 boat dives
- Total: 1.5 days

### Fun Dives
- **AM boat:** meet 7:00am, return ~12:30pm (2 dives — Manta Point + Crystal Bay)
- **PM boat:** meet 12:15pm, return ~4:30pm (2 dives — northern/eastern sites)
- Client can choose AM, PM, or both

### Deep Adventure + Fun Dive
- Same schedule as Fun Dives — AM or PM
- 2 dives: 1 deep dive (30m) + 1 fun dive

### Nitrox Specialty
- **Day 1:** classroom + 2 boat dives with Nitrox (usually AM boat)
- Total: 1 day

### Nitrox Dry (theory only)
- **Half day:** classroom + exam, no diving
- Can be scheduled around any other activity

### Deep Specialty
- **Day 1 & Day 2:** 2 boat dives per day reaching max 40m
- Total: 2 days

### React Right (dry course)
- **1 day:** classroom + practical scenarios, no water session
- Independent of boat schedule — can be done any day

### Stress & Rescue Course
- **Day 1:** theory class + pool skills (PM)
- **Day 2:** 2 boat dives — open water rescue scenarios
- **Day 3:** 2 boat dives + written exam
- Total: 3 consecutive days

### Basic Diver → OW Upgrade
- **Day 1 (PM):** theory review + pool
- **Day 2 (AM):** 3 boat dives (7:00am to 12:30pm)
- Total: 2 days

### Scuba Diver → OW Upgrade
- **Day 1 (PM):** theory review + pool
- **Day 2 (AM):** 2 boat dives
- Total: 2 days

## LOGISTICS — PRE-COURSE AND ARRIVAL

### Day before the activity starts:
- Client should visit DPM dive center to register and verify gear sizes
- Office hours: 7:00am – 7:00pm
- This step is **strongly recommended** but not mandatory if impossible

### Same-day arrival (no day-before visit):
- Client can come by the morning of activity, before it starts
- For AM boat: arrive at the center by 6:30am to allow time for paperwork

### Luggage:
- Can be left at the dive center
- Showers and bathrooms available at the center
- No lockers — staff watches over bags

### What to bring:
- Swimwear, sunscreen (reef-safe preferred), towel (although DPM provides one)
- Any prescription medication
- Passport or ID (for SSI certification paperwork)
- Camera/GoPro if wanted (rental available)

### What's included in all dive programs:
- Full dive gear (wetsuit, BCD, regulator, tank, fins, mask)
- Professional instructor or dive guide
- SSI dive insurance
- Snacks on board (cookies, fruit, water, coffee, tea)
- Boat transfers to all dive sites
- Shower and towel service at the dive center

## 24-HOUR FLIGHT RULE — CRITICAL

**Always inform clients with flights within 24 hours of their last dive.**

- Minimum 24 hours after the last dive before flying
- This is a safety standard — non-negotiable
- If timing doesn't work, adjust the program (start one day earlier, skip the last dive day, etc.)

Example check:
- Client books 3-day OW + has a flight Day 4 at 9am → flight is less than 24h after Day 3 dives → suggest moving flight or rearranging program

Frase oficial:
- EN: "Just a heads up — there must be at least 24 hours between your last dive and any flight ✈️ Want me to check if your schedule works?"
- ES: "Tené en cuenta que tiene que haber mínimo 24 horas entre tu última inmersión y cualquier vuelo ✈️ ¿Querés que verifique si los tiempos te dan?"

## BOOKING TIMING RECOMMENDATIONS

- **High season** (Jul–Sep, Dec–Jan): book 2-4 weeks ahead minimum to guarantee dates
- **Low season** (Feb–Jun, Oct–Nov): often possible to book within the week
- **Walk-ins:** possible but never guaranteed — always check roster live
- **Same-day bookings:** see "Today Availability Logic" section above

Use natural urgency phrases when relevant:
- EN: "Boat spaces fill up quickly, especially for the morning Manta trip — booking ahead guarantees your preferred dates 🤿"
- ES: "Los cupos del barco se llenan rápido, sobre todo el de la mañana con Mantas — reservar con anticipación te garantiza tus fechas 🤿"
