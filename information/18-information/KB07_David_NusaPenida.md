# KB-07 | FAQs AND SPECIAL CASES — DPM DIVING NUSA PENIDA

---

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Can I do the Open Water course with no experience?
Yes. The Open Water and OW30 courses are specifically designed for beginners — no previous experience is required. Basic swimming ability is required (200m swim + 10 minutes floating).

### Can I finish the Open Water course in less than 3 days?
No. The Open Water and OW30 courses are 3 consecutive days minimum. The structure (theory → pool → confined water → open water dives) cannot be compressed safely.

### Open Water vs Open Water 30 — what's the difference?
Same 3 days, same structure, same number of dives (6). Differences:
- **Depth:** OW Conv = 18m max | OW30 = 30m max
- **Certifications:** OW Conv = SSI Open Water | OW30 = SSI Open Water + SSI Deep Adventurer recognition
- **Inclusions:** OW30 adds free T-shirt + mask & snorkel + reusable bottle
- **Cross-branch benefit:** OW30 includes 50% off Fun Dives at all other DPM branches (one-time per location)
- **Price:** OW Conv = 6,400,000 IDR | OW30 = 9,500,000 IDR

### I wear prescription glasses — can I dive?
Yes. Either:
- Wear contact lenses (perfectly safe)
- Or use a prescription (graded) mask — availability not guaranteed, ask at the dive center first

### Is food included in the programs?
No meals included. Snacks are provided on board: cookies, fruit, coffee, tea, water. Clients can bring their own light snacks. There are restaurants near the dive center for breakfast/lunch/dinner.

### I'm arriving today — can I dive today?
It depends on arrival time and roster availability:
- Before 7:00am Bali time → potentially AM boat (check roster)
- 7:00am – 12:15pm → potentially PM boat (check roster)
- After 12:15pm → both boats already left → tomorrow AM (offer Manta Point)

Always check the roster endpoint before quoting "yes today is possible."

### How many students per group?
Maximum 4 divers per instructor for all certification courses. For Fun Dives the ratio is typically 1 dive guide per 4-6 divers (relaxed for certified divers).

### Can I pay by card?
Not in Nusa Penida — there is no card terminal. Deposit by Wise / Revolut / N26 (no fee) or cash IDR at office. Balance on arrival by cash IDR (preferred) or transfer (+3% surcharge).

### What are the usual dive sites?
- **AM boat (7:00am):** Manta Point + Crystal Bay (south + west of the island) — best for mantas and Mola Mola
- **PM boat (12:15pm):** Sental / Toyapakeh / SD Point / Buyuk / Ped (north + east of the island) — drift dives over coral reefs

### Can I take a flight after diving?
No. Wait minimum 24 hours after your last dive before any flight. This is a safety standard — non-negotiable.

### Can I pay the full amount upfront before arriving?
Yes — via bank transfer in EUR/USD/GBP/AUD using the same Wise accounts. No 3% surcharge if paid by Wise/Revolut. The deposit is deducted from the total.

### I did my Open Water at another DPM branch — do I get a discount?
Yes — automatic 5% loyalty discount on any program. Offer proactively without waiting for the client to ask.
If you have an Open Water 30 from another DPM branch → 50% off Fun Dives in Nusa Penida (one-time, verify certification card).

### I have a PADI certification — can I take courses with DPM?
Yes. We are SSI, but we accept all certification agencies (PADI, NAUI, BSAC, etc.) for any program. Your level transfers directly. Note: upgrades within DPM (Basic→OW, Scuba→OW) only work for SSI cards from DPM.

### Are PADI and SSI different?
No — same lifetime international certification, recognized worldwide. SSI and PADI follow the same global standards. If a client mentions PADI, clarify:
- EN: "We're SSI — same worldwide lifetime certification as PADI, recognized everywhere 🤿"
- ES: "Somos SSI — la misma certificación internacional y de por vida que PADI, válida en todo el mundo 🤿"

### I don't know how to swim — can I do the Open Water course?
If the client cannot swim at all → recommend Try Scuba (1.5 days, max 12m, no swimming required) or Scuba Diver (1.5 days, cert to 12m, no swimming required).
If the client can swim a little → can attempt Open Water if willing to practice basic swimming before arriving.

### My friend is certified, I'm not — can we dive together?
This is the **grupo mixto** scenario. Read KB-11 §12 for full handling.

Short version:
- **Different programs = same boat, different groups.** Each has their own instructor/guide, depth, and pace. Not "diving together" in the strict sense.
- **If they really want to dive side-by-side:** the certified diver can join the Try Scuba group with one single guide. The certified diver agrees to stay at the Try Scuba max depth (12m). One guide for both. Certified diver pays Fun Dive rate (1,800,000 IDR).
- **If they have time and budget:** OW30 for the beginner + Fun Dives or Refresh + Deep Adventure for the certified → upsell chain (see KB-11 §12.6).

### My friend is not a diver — can they come on the boat to watch?
No. Only divers are allowed on the boat for safety reasons.
Non-divers can stay at the hostel or explore the island during the activity.

### Do you have a pickup and drop-off service?
No. The meeting point is the dive center at Banjar Nyuh. Clients arrange their own transport.
Recommended taxi: **Oboss Sugik +62 877-6075-4969** (WhatsApp).

### Are photos and videos included?
No — photos and videos are not included in any program. Clients can rent a GoPro at the dive center:
- GoPro: 350,000 IDR/day
- GoPro accessories: 150,000 IDR
Note: For Try Scuba and Scuba Diver, the student typically cannot operate their own camera underwater — instructors can take a few photos.

### Do you offer snorkeling?
No. DPM Nusa Penida is a scuba diving operation only.
If a group has divers + non-divers wanting to snorkel → suggest the divers do their program with us, and the non-divers book a separate snorkel tour locally (the hostel or office can recommend). Snorkeling + diving on the same boat is NOT possible.

### Do you go to Manta Bay (snorkel-only manta spot)?
No — we go to **Manta Point** (the diving site, deeper). Manta Bay is a shallow snorkel-only area, which is a different operation. For diving with mantas → Manta Point is the place, and it's exclusively AM boat.

### Do you offer night dives?
No — DPM Nusa Penida does not offer night dives.
If a client specifically wants night diving → recommend **Koh Tao branch** and offer to transfer the conversation to Emma.

### Do you go to wreck dives?
No — there are no wreck dive sites in Nusa Penida.
If a client specifically wants wreck dives → recommend **Koh Tao** (Sattakut wreck) and offer to transfer to Emma.

### Can I do a single dive?
No — all our programs include minimum 2 dives per outing.

### I want to dive tomorrow but arriving from Lombok / Gili — is it possible?
Depends on ferry timing. Ferries from Lombok/Gili to Nusa Penida usually require a connection in Bali (Padangbai). Allow at least 1 full day for transit.
For day-of-arrival diving from any other island → unlikely to work with the ferry schedules. Recommend arriving day before.

### Cancellation policy?
- Deposit is non-refundable
- Reservation can be changed to a different date in Nusa Penida → no charge
- Reservation can be transferred to any other DPM branch → no charge
- Full refund only in case of operational failure on DPM's side (boat cancellation, etc. — rare)

### How long in advance should I book?
- **High season** (Jul-Sep, Dec-Jan): 2-4 weeks ahead recommended
- **Low season** (Feb-Jun, Oct-Nov): often possible within the week
- **Same-day:** possible but never guaranteed — always check live availability

## SPECIAL CASES

### Children and Minimum Ages
- **Minimum age for all programs:** 10 years old
- **Ages 8-9:** May be considered for Try Scuba at maximum depth 5m — always check with office first
- **Junior Open Water (age 10-14):** Certifies to maximum 12m, automatically upgrades to full Open Water (18m) at age 15
- **OW30 minimum age:** 10 (same as Junior OW depth restrictions apply)

### Non-swimmers and weak swimmers
- Try Scuba and Scuba Diver: no swimming required, instructor present at all times
- Open Water and OW30: basic swimming ability required (200m swim + 10 min float)
- Always ask politely when relevant:
  - EN: "How would you rate your comfort in the water from 1 to 5?"
  - ES: "¿Cómo describirías tu comodidad en el agua del 1 al 5?"

### Certified diver, last dive more than 12 months ago
- **Refresh + 2 Dives is mandatory** (2,100,000 IDR) — safety requirement, not optional
- Upsell to **Refresh + Deep Adventure combo** (Refresh + Deep Adventure card via DA+FD package, total ~4,700,000 IDR) — see KB-10 and KB-11
- Exception: Divemaster and Instructor do not need a Refresh

### Customer asks for a specific instructor
- Never make promises
- EN: "We can try to arrange it, but I can't guarantee a specific instructor 🙏"
- ES: "Vamos a tratar de arreglarlo, pero no puedo garantizar un instructor específico 🙏"
- Add note in conversation and escalate to office if client insists

### Buddy bookings (client wants to be paired with another booked diver)
- Escalate to [AGENTE REQUERIDO:buddy-cliente:X,amigo:X,programa:X,fecha:X]
- Office handles buddy pairing

### Transfer of dives between people
- Escalate to office — not handled by David
- Example: original client cancelled, gives the booking to a friend

### Advanced dive customization
- The 5 Advanced dives are typically: Perfect Buoyancy, Navigation, Deep Dive, Drift Diving, Fish ID
- Can be customized (e.g., replace one with Underwater Naturalist or Wreck) — confirm with office before committing

### Medical conditions
- Do NOT ask preemptively
- If client mentions a condition (asthma, claustrophobia, diabetes, heart issue, etc.):
  - EN: "Thanks for letting me know. We'd recommend a quick check with a local doctor on arrival — we can recommend a clinic on the island. With the doctor's clearance you can dive as planned 😊"
  - ES: "Gracias por avisarme. Te recomendamos pasar por una clínica local al llegar para una evaluación rápida — te recomendamos una que conocemos. Con el OK del doctor podés bucear sin problema 😊"
- Add tag `MEDICO:[condición]`
- Escalate to [AGENTE REQUERIDO]
- Mention the medical form: "Medical form when you arrive" / "Formulario médico al llegar"

### Age 45+
- Same protocol as medical: medical form required on arrival
- Frase:
  - EN: "Just a quick note — for divers 45 and up, we do a short medical form on arrival. Standard safety check, nothing to worry about 😊"
  - ES: "Solo un detalle — para buceadores de 45+ hacemos un formulario médico al llegar. Es un chequeo de seguridad estándar, nada de qué preocuparse 😊"

### Pregnant clients
- Diving is NOT recommended during pregnancy
- Politely decline:
  - EN: "Diving isn't recommended during pregnancy 🙏 We'd love to dive with you another time. In the meantime, snorkeling tours are available with other local operators."
  - ES: "Bucear no se recomienda durante el embarazo 🙏 Nos encantaría que bucees con nosotros en otro momento. Mientras tanto hay tours de snorkel disponibles con operadores locales."

### Client requesting refund
- Escalate to office — David does not handle refunds
- Frase: "Let me connect you with our office to handle this 🙏" / "Voy a conectarte con la oficina para resolver esto 🙏"

### Hostel negotiation (price, room type)
- Hostel rates are fixed: 150K divers / 350K non-divers
- Cannot negotiate price — escalate if client insists on private/different room

### Group 8+ people
- Escalate to office for group rate and logistics
- David handles up to 7 people, beyond that requires manual coordination

### DM / Instructor inquiries
- Pass office phone: +62 812-3769-3299
- Don't give details about cost, duration, or schedule

### Customer wants to extend stay beyond original dates
- Confirm with office before committing
- Hostel rate same: 150K/350K per night

### Same client booking multiple programs over multiple days
- David can manage 2-3 programs for the same client (e.g., OW + Fun Dives after)
- Beyond that → consult office for coordination

## WHAT WE DO NOT OFFER IN NUSA PENIDA

- Night dives → Koh Tao
- Snorkeling tours
- Single dives (minimum 2 dives per trip)
- Beach dives (all dives are boat dives)
- Wreck dives → Koh Tao (Sattakut)
- Whale shark trips → Koh Tao (Chumphon Pinnacle)
- Photos/videos as a service
- Pickup or drop-off service
- Private rooms at our hostel (Penida Patio is shared dorm only)
- Non-divers on the boat
- Pool-only courses (no shop-only programs without boat)
- Liveaboards
