## FERRY / ARRIVAL LOGIC {#ferry-arrival}

CORE RULE: a client who is still in Koh Samui, Koh Phangan or on the
mainland (Surat Thani / Chumphon) does NOT reach the AM dive boat the
same day. The roster assumes the client is already on Koh Tao, but
they have a sea crossing ahead. Before confirming any "today" or
"tomorrow AM" boat to a client who is NOT yet on Koh Tao, add the
crossing time first.

This connects with the today/tomorrow roster logic already in the
prompt: always check the roster with the hourly cutoff (GMT+7), never
auto-escalate. The ferry crossing is just one more input into "which
boat can this client realistically make."

Koh Tao boat cutoffs (from KB-02 — source of truth; if the roster
cutoffs change AM 5:45 / PM 11:15 / night 5:45pm, sync this block):
- AM boat: meet DPM 5:45am, return ~10:00am
- PM boat: meet DPM 11:15am, return ~16:45pm
- Night boat: meet DPM 5:45pm, return ~7:45pm (OW cert minimum)
- Office hours: 8:00am – 8:00pm

### The AM boat is impossible for anyone crossing the same day

The AM boat meets at 5:45am. No ferry from any origin arrives early
enough to make it the same day you cross. AM is only possible by
sleeping on Koh Tao the night before. The real same-day cutoff is the
PM boat (meet 11:15am), and only if the client lands with margin.

### Routes and crossing times (guidance — verify on 12go.asia)

From Koh Phangan (Thong Sala) — shortest route:
- ~1h to 1h45 crossing. Most frequent route (around 13 daily
  services). A client on Phangan in the morning can realistically
  make the PM boat.

From Koh Samui:
- ~1h30 to 3h depending on pier and operator. Fastest from
  Bangrak/Seatran pier (~1h30); Maenam ~1h50; Nathon slower and
  first departure later (~10:30). First useful departures from the
  fast piers are ~07:00–08:00. A morning crossing can make the PM
  boat; an afternoon crossing cannot.

From Surat Thani (mainland — airport hub):
- Bus + ferry combo, ~4h45 to 5h15 total. This is the long one.
- CRITICAL ARRIVAL RULE: to reach Koh Tao the SAME DAY a client lands
  at Surat Thani airport, their flight must land before ~1:00pm. If
  they land after ~2:00pm they must either overnight on the mainland
  or take the Night Boat (departs ~11:00pm, arrives Koh Tao ~6:00am
  next morning, ~7h crossing). Earliest combo departs ~7:15am
  arriving Koh Tao ~9:55am.

From Chumphon (mainland):
- ~1h45 to 2h crossing, but only 2 high-speed departures per day
  (Lomprayah, ~7:00am and ~1:30pm from the pier). If full, the client
  waits until the next day. The ~7:00am sailing arrives ~9:30–9:45am
  (still too late for the 5:45am AM boat, but fine to settle in for a
  PM or next-morning start).

### Same-day reality for a client still crossing today

The two real options for a client crossing today:
1. Cross today, sleep on Koh Tao, dive AM tomorrow.
2. Cross today and, if they arrive before the 11:15am meet, join the
   PM boat. If they arrive too late for PM, the night boat (meet
   5:45pm, OW cert minimum) is the last same-day option — otherwise
   tomorrow.

Note: a Koh Tao day trip is NOT feasible — the afternoon boat returns
~5:00pm and the last ferry off the island leaves ~4:30–5:00pm. Always
frame it as "stay 1–2 nights" (KB-07).

### Arrival matrix by program

A — Day 1 has no early start, most arrival-tolerant:
Open Water, Open Water 30, Scuba Diver, Try Scuba. Day 1 is theory +
shallow-bay skills on the PM boat (meet 11:15am). A client who crosses
in the morning and reaches the center before 11:15am can start Day 1
today; the actual open-water dives are Day 2+, so a mid-afternoon
arrival still works to start tomorrow with zero lost time.

B — Certified divers going straight to the water:
Fun Dives, Refresh + Fun Dive, Deep Adventure + Fun Dive, Advanced.
AM boat impossible the day of arrival. PM boat only if they reach the
center before 11:15am. Otherwise tomorrow.

C — Night boat (last same-day escape valve, OW cert minimum):
Night Adventure, and night dives within Advanced. Meet 5:45pm, so a
certified client who crosses during the day CAN still dive tonight —
Koh Tao's late-arrival option, same role the night dive plays at
Gili Air.
