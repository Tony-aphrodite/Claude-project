### Conventional Open Water Course
- Price: 11,000 THB per person
- Negotiation floor: 10,000 THB (first offer) — absolute minimum: 9,500 THB
- Duration: 3 days (can be done in 2 days — same price, but only 4 dives instead of 6)
- Minimum age: 10 years old
- Max depth: 18 meters (Junior OW for under-15s certifies to 12m, auto-upgrades to 18m at age 15)
- Certification: SSI Open Water (international and lifetime)
- Inclusions: SSI Open Water certification, professional instructor, full gear, dive insurance, 6 boat dives, DPM T-shirt or reusable eco bottle, snacks on board
- Accommodation option: +1,000 THB for 3 nights in shared room (subject to availability)
- Catalog code EN: dozxkzuo7z | ES: o9puu1mhzu

## PROGRAMS FOR CERTIFIED DIVERS

### Fun Dives
- Price: 2,100 THB per trip
- Includes 2 boat dives per trip
- Requirement: any diving certification (Scuba Diver, Open Water, Advanced or higher)
- Requirement: must have dived within the last 6 months — otherwise Refresh is required
- Catalog code EN: abf12s6boz | ES: 2nvxnfib66

### Refresh + Fun Dive
- Price: 2,600 THB per person
- Required when certified diver has not dived in more than 6 months
- Requirement: Scuba Diver, Open Water, Advanced or Rescue certification
- Note: Divemaster and Instructor do not need the Refresh
- Inclusions: theory review, shallow skills dive, 1 fun dive, pro dive guide, full gear, dive insurance, snacks on board
- Catalog code EN: (Refresh + Fun Dive KT) | ES: 6oz81kymrs

### Adventure Dives
- Price: 2,900 THB per adventure dive
- Since DPM does not do single dives, a Fun Dive is added: total 3,900 THB
- Exception: Night Adventure — single dive format, 2,900 THB
- Requirement: Open Water certification minimum
- Each adventure dive awards a lifetime SSI recognition card for that specific dive
- Available adventures: Perfect Buoyancy, Navigation, Deep Dive, Wreck Dive, Night Dive, Fish ID, Nitrox
- Most popular: Deep Adventure (dive to 30 meters even with OW certification)
- Catalog code EN: 0r997i5f2w | ES: zt535965g1

### Deep Adventure + Fun Dive (package)
- Price: 3,900 THB
- Includes: SSI Deep Adventure recognition card, 2 boat dives, pro instructor, full gear, dive insurance, snacks on board

### Advanced Course
- Price: 10,000 THB per person
- Discount: 10% (9,000 THB) if the student completed their Open Water with DPM
- Duration: 2 days
- Requirement: Open Water Diver certification
- Max depth after completion: 30 meters
- No theory class or written exams — fully practical course
- Includes 5 dives: Perfect Buoyancy, Navigation, Deep Dive, Wreck Dive, Night Dive
- Note: if student has already done some of these dives, they can be replaced with others (e.g. Fish ID, Nitrox)
- Inclusions: SSI Advanced Adventurer certification, pro instructor, full gear, dive insurance, 5 boat dives, snacks on board
- Catalog code EN: s0j4jxp39m | ES: yrt1o03ncp

### Night Adventure Dive
