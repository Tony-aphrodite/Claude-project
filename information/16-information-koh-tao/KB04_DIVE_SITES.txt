# KB-04 | DIVE SITES — DPM DIVING KOH TAO

All dive sites listed are our usual sites. Dive sites are always subject to change depending on weather and sea conditions. Final sites are confirmed the day before based on conditions.

---

## MORNING BOAT DIVE SITES

### Sattakut (Shipwreck)
- Travel time: 10–15 minutes
- Level: Advanced and higher
- Visibility: 10–30 meters
- Water temperature: 27–30°C
- Marine life: Hard and soft corals on the wreck, snappers, fusiliers, barracudas, batfish, lionfish, groupers

### White Rock
- Travel time: 10–15 minutes
- Level: All levels
- Visibility: 10–25 meters
- Water temperature: 28–30°C
- Marine life: Sea turtle, titan triggerfish, angelfish, blue spotted stingray, banded sea snake, scribbled filefish, great barracuda

### Twins
- Travel time: 10–15 minutes
- Level: All levels
- Visibility: 15–30 meters
- Water temperature: 28–30°C
- Marine life: Saddleback anemonefish, gobies, blue spotted stingray, white eyed moray eels, yellow boxfish, titan triggerfish

### Suphairin 313 (Shipwreck)
- Morning boat site
- Level: All levels

### Chumphon Pinnacle — SPECIAL SITE
- Travel time: 45 minutes
- Level: Open Water and higher (recommended for Advanced and higher)
- Visibility: 15–30 meters
- Water temperature: 28–30°C
- Marine life: Whale shark, razorfish, longfin batfish, giant grouper, great barracuda, lionfish, scorpionfish, pink anemonefish
- Schedule: approximately once a week — no fixed schedule, always check with office

### Southwest Pinnacle
- Morning boat (occasional)
- Level: Advanced recommended

---

## AFTERNOON BOAT DIVE SITES (Koh Nang Yuan Park area)

### Japanese Garden
- Travel time: 15 minutes
- Level: All levels
- Visibility: 20–30 meters
- Water temperature: 28–30°C
- Marine life: Funnel corals, mushroom corals, angelfish, titan triggerfish, porcupine pufferfish

### Mango Bay
- Travel time: 15 minutes
- Level: All levels (recommended for beginners)
- Visibility: 15–30 meters
- Water temperature: 28–30°C
- Marine life: Hard coral, fusiliers, Jenkins ray, blue spotted stingray, titan triggerfish, parrotfish, common seahorse

### Red Rock
- Afternoon boat site
- Level: All levels

### Chinese Wall
- Afternoon boat site
- Level: All levels

---

## SITES WE DO NOT VISIT
- Sail Rock — DPM Koh Tao does not go to Sail Rock
- If a customer asks about Sail Rock, offer the Sattakut Wreck as an alternative wreck dive option

---

## GENERAL WATER CONDITIONS
- Typical visibility: 15 meters or more
- Water temperature: 27–30°C year-round
- Dive sites around Koh Nang Yuan Park (afternoon) have low current risk
- Any concerns about conditions can be addressed with: "We have been having great dives. Our typical visibility is 15 meters or more, and the afternoon sites around Koh Nang Yuan have minimal current."
