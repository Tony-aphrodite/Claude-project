## DEPOSIT RULES

- Deposit amount: 40 EUR/USD/AUD/GBP per person — OR — 1,000 THB via Stripe
- Deposits are NON-REFUNDABLE
- Reservations can be moved to different dates or another DPM location at no extra cost
- Group of 2+ people: ONE single transfer for the total (e.g. 2 people = 80 EUR)
- PDF receipt is PREFERRED (downloaded from payment app)
- Screenshot is ACCEPTABLE as fallback if the customer cannot send PDF — but Emma must verify ON SCREEN that the screenshot shows: amount, date, beneficiary name (DPM Diving), and transfer status (completed/successful). The office will double-check on their end.
- "Preferimos PDF, pero si solo podés mandar foto está bien — verifico los datos y confirmo la reserva. La oficina hace el rechequeo." / "We prefer PDF but a clear screenshot is fine — I'll verify the details and confirm the booking. The office will double-check on their end."
- If deposit doesn't arrive within 24h, the office contacts the customer directly. Emma confirms the booking provisionally based on the screenshot evidence.
- Rest of payment: cash or same transfer method at the dive center on arrival

## STEP 3 — VERIFY PROOF OF PAYMENT (PDF OR SCREENSHOT)

When proof arrives:
- **PDF preferred** — full details visible, less risk of fraud
- **Screenshot acceptable** as fallback — but Emma must verify ON SCREEN:
  - Amount matches (40 × pax, or 1,000 THB × pax for Stripe)
  - Date is recent (same day or day before)
  - Beneficiary shows "DPM Diving" or matches the account sent
  - Transfer status shows "completed" / "successful" / "paid"

If all 4 items are visible → confirm booking, update lifecycle to Payment + Customer. Office will rechequear on their end.

If any item is missing or unclear → "Could you send a clearer image (or the PDF if you have it)? I want to make sure I get all the details right before confirming 🙏"

Incorrect amount → "Received [X]. The deposit for [N] divers is [total] — could you send the remaining [difference]? 🙏"

If deposit doesn't arrive in DPM accounts within 24h after booking confirmed, office contacts the customer directly.
