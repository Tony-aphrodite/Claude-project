# KB-03 | ACCOMMODATION — DPM DIVING KOH TAO

IMPORTANT: All accommodation is subject to availability. Always verify with the office before confirming to any customer.

---

## ACCOMMODATION AT DPM HOSTEL

### Room Types
- Shared room for 4 people
- Shared room for 12 people
- No private rooms available

### Features
- Air conditioning (AC)
- No hot water (not needed — Koh Tao is hot all year round)
- Clean and comfortable
- Located inside the dive center

### Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in: 1:00 PM
- Check-out: 10:30 AM

### Key and Towel Deposit
- 500 THB refundable deposit for key and towel upon check-in (do not mention to customers — internal information only)

---

## ACCOMMODATION PRICES BY PROGRAM

### Open Water Course (Conventional) and Open Water 30
- Special package: add 3 nights for only 1,000 THB (included with the program price)
- **OW + 3 nights = 12,000 THB total**
- **OW30 + 3 nights = 18,900 THB total**
- This is an offer exclusive to OW and OW30 students
- Subject to availability — always verify before confirming

### Try Scuba Diving, Scuba Diver, Advanced Course, Rescue Course, Specialty Courses
- Price: 500 THB per person per night
- Subject to availability — always verify before confirming

### Fun Dives and Refresh
- DPM Koh Tao does not offer accommodation for these programs
- Direct customers to booking.com to find accommodation on the island

### Divemaster and Instructor Course
- Accommodation is included in the program price (44,000 THB includes stay at DPM Hostel during training)

### All other programs not listed above
- No accommodation available through DPM

## IMPORTANT NOTES
- Non-divers and companions are NOT allowed on the boat for safety reasons.
- Companions who do not dive can stay at the hostel but cannot participate in dive activities.
- Cannot guarantee assignment to a specific room size (4-person or 12-person dorm).
- In low season, requests for the 4-person dorm may be accommodated — always check availability.
- Meals are not provided. Snacks are available on the boat (cookies, fruit, coffee, tea, water). Customers may bring light snacks.
