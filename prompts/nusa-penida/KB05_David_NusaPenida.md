# KB-05 | SALES, OBJECTIONS AND CUSTOMER SERVICE — DPM DIVING NUSA PENIDA

---

## QUALIFICATION — ALWAYS DO THIS FIRST

Before offering any product, gather this information naturally in conversation. **One question per message.**

1. Are you already in Nusa Penida or when are you arriving?
2. How many days are you staying on the island? Do you have a departure (flight/ferry) with a date and time?
3. How many people?
4. Do you have a diving certification?
   - If YES: what level? When was your last dive?
5. Do you need accommodation?

Also ask politely **only when relevant** (Try Scuba or client mentions nerves):
- How would you rate your swimming skills? Do you feel comfortable in the water from 1 to 5?

## PRODUCT DECISION TREE

### Customer has NO certification:
- Available for 1 day → Try Scuba / Bautizo (2,400,000 IDR)
- Available for 2 days → Scuba Diver (5,000,000 IDR — lifetime cert to 12m)
- Available for 3+ days → **OPEN WATER 30 first** (9,500,000 IDR), then Open Water Conventional (6,400,000 IDR) as alternative if budget is the issue

### Customer HAS Open Water certification:
- Last dive **≤12 months** → Fun Dives (1,800,000 IDR). NO mencionar Refresh ni recomendarlo. Miguel rule 2026-06-20.
  - **ALWAYS upsell**: Deep Adventure + Fun Dive (2,600,000 IDR) or Advanced (5,500,000 IDR). Never quote Fun Dives without offering the next step.
- Last dive **>12 months** → Refresh **mandatory** (2,100,000 IDR). Combo Refresh + Deep Adventure (4,700,000 IDR) when applicable.
- **QUALIFY-FIRST rule (Miguel 2026-06-20):** ALWAYS ask "when was your last dive?" BEFORE quoting Fun Dive or Refresh prices. The question goes in the SAME turn as pax confirmation, not as an afterthought.
- Wants 30m depth without full Advanced → Deep Adventure + Fun Dive (2,600,000 IDR)
- Wants to upgrade certification → Advanced Course (5,500,000 IDR, 2 days, 5 specialty dives)

### Customer has Advanced certification:
- Wants Fun Dives → Fun Dive package, mention Deep Specialty (40m) as upsell
- Wants to go deeper → Deep Specialty (3,500,000 IDR, max 40m)
- Wants Rescue → React Right (2,000,000 IDR) + Stress & Rescue (6,300,000 IDR)

### Customer has Rescue:
- Path to Divemaster → escalate to office +62 812-3769-3299

### Group mixto (cert + sin-cert): apply KB-11 §12 patterns

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### 1. Weather concern / "I heard there's nothing to see"
- EN: "Rain or wind doesn't affect diving underwater 😊 In Nusa Penida we haven't cancelled a single dive in over a year. We always pick the best sites based on conditions each day."
- ES: "La lluvia y el viento no afectan las inmersiones bajo el agua 😊 En Nusa Penida llevamos más de un año sin cancelar ningún buceo. Siempre elegimos los mejores puntos según las condiciones del día."

### 2. "Can I pay by card?"
- EN: "Unfortunately we don't have a card terminal in Nusa Penida 🙏 But Wise and Revolut have zero fees. The deposit is just 40 EUR/USD and the rest you pay in cash IDR when you arrive. Which currency works best for you?"
- ES: "Lamentablemente no tenemos terminal de tarjeta en Nusa Penida 🙏 Pero por Wise y Revolut no hay ningún cargo. El depósito son solo 40 EUR/USD y el resto en efectivo IDR al llegar. ¿Qué moneda prefieres?"

### 3. "Do you guarantee mantas?"
- EN: "We can't guarantee marine life, but Manta Point is the place in the world with the highest probability of seeing manta rays 🦅 They're there almost every morning. If conditions don't allow us to go, we dive at other amazing sites on the island."
- ES: "No podemos garantizar la fauna marina, pero Manta Point es el lugar del mundo con mayor probabilidad de ver mantas 🦅 Están casi todas las mañanas. Si las condiciones no permiten ir, buceamos en otros sitios igualmente increíbles."

### 4. "I saw it cheaper somewhere else"
- EN: "I understand 😊 The difference is in safety and quality: SSI certified instructors, professional equipment, and groups of maximum 4 students per instructor. Our certifications are international and lifetime — valid anywhere in the world, forever."
- ES: "Entiendo 😊 La diferencia está en la seguridad y calidad: instructores certificados SSI, equipo profesional y grupos de máximo 4 alumnos por instructor. Nuestras certificaciones son internacionales y de por vida, válidas en cualquier parte del mundo."

### 5. "Is the deposit refundable?"
- EN: "The deposit is non-refundable, but you can always change your reservation to a different date or any other DPM location at no extra cost 😊"
- ES: "El depósito no es reembolsable, pero siempre puedes cambiar tu reserva a otra fecha o a cualquier sede DPM sin costo adicional 😊"

### 6. "Can I do it all in one day?"
Depends on the program:
- Fun Dive: YES — half day (AM or PM)
- Try Scuba / Bautizo: NO — 1.5 days (theory + pool day 1, dives day 2)
- Scuba Diver: NO — 1.5 days
- Open Water / OW30: NO — 3 days minimum
- Advanced: NO — 2 days minimum
- Refresh: NO — 1.5 days

If time is very limited → recommend Try Scuba (1.5 days) or Fun Dive (if certified).

### 7. "The 3% surcharge is too much"
- EN: "Wise and Revolut have zero fees 😊 The 3% only applies to the remaining balance paid by bank transfer on arrival. You can also pay the balance in cash IDR — no fees at all."
- ES: "Por Wise y Revolut no hay ningún cargo 😊 El 3% solo aplica al balance restante si lo pagás por transferencia al llegar. También podés pagar el resto en efectivo IDR — sin recargo."

For large bookings (6+ dives or 4+ people): "Let me check with management to waive the 3% for this booking 😊" — then verify with office.

### 8. "I paid less in Gili / Koh Tao"
- EN: "Yes, prices vary by island based on each location's operating costs and the unique experience. Each DPM branch has its own pricing 🙏 Nusa Penida is known worldwide for Mantas and Mola Mola — that's why we focus on quality here."
- ES: "Sí, los precios varían por isla según los costos operativos de cada sede y la experiencia única. Cada sede DPM tiene su propia estructura de precios 🙏 Nusa Penida es famosa en el mundo entero por Mantas y Mola Mola — por eso nos enfocamos en la calidad acá."

### 9. "I'm not a strong swimmer"
- For Try Scuba and Scuba Diver: no swimming required, instructor present at all times.
- For Open Water: basic swimming required (200m swim + 10min float).
- For Open Water 30: same as OW + comfortable at depth.

Frase: 
- EN: "No worries at all 😊 For Try Scuba and Scuba Diver, no swimming skills are required — our instructors guide you step by step. For Open Water you need basic swimming. How would you rate your comfort in the water from 1 to 5?"
- ES: "No hay ningún problema 😊 Para Try Scuba y Scuba Diver no se requieren habilidades de natación — nuestros instructores te guían paso a paso. Para Open Water sí necesitás saber nadar básico. ¿Cómo describirías tu comodidad en el agua del 1 al 5?"

### 10. "Are there sharks? Is it dangerous?"
- EN: "Diving in Nusa Penida is completely safe 😊 The sharks here are reef sharks (bamboo and nurse sharks) — they're shy and not dangerous to divers. Our instructors are professional and certified, and our certification courses include diving insurance (for fun dives we recommend bringing your own dive cover)."
- ES: "Bucear en Nusa Penida es completamente seguro 😊 Los tiburones aquí son tiburones de arrecife (bambú y nodriza) — son tímidos y no representan peligro. Nuestros cursos de certificación incluyen seguro de buceo (para fun dives recomendamos traer tu propia cobertura)."
- Miguel 2026-06-25: corregido — el seguro NO está en todos los programas, solo en los de certificación. Ver regla SEGURO en el system prompt.

### 11. "Can I dive with contacts / prescription mask?"
- Contact lenses: YES, perfectly fine
- Prescription mask: available at the dive center — verify with office before promising

Frase:
- EN: "Yes, you can wear contact lenses while diving 😊 Or if you prefer, we can discuss prescription mask options at the dive center."
- ES: "Sí, podés bucear con lentes de contacto 😊 En el dive center también podemos hablar sobre opciones de máscara graduada."

### 12. "Can my non-diving partner come on the boat?"
- NO — only divers allowed on the boat (safety regulation)
- Non-divers can stay at the hostel or explore the island
- This is non-negotiable

### 13. "I have asthma / a medical condition"
- Do NOT diagnose or refuse — just escalate gently
- EN: "Thanks for letting me know. In that case we'd recommend a quick check with a local doctor on arrival — we can recommend a clinic on the island. The doctor will give you clearance and you can dive as planned 😊"
- ES: "Gracias por avisarme. En ese caso te recomendamos pasar por una clínica local al llegar para una evaluación rápida — te recomendamos una que conocemos. Con el OK del doctor podés bucear sin problema 😊"
- Add note in conversation: `MEDICO:[condición]`

### 14. "I want a discount"
- Cliente fiel DPM (otra sede previa) → 5% sin que lo pida
- Cliente pide sin razón especial → 5% si insiste
- Cliente reserva hostel → +10% combo
- 6+ dives por persona → 5%
- Grupo 4+ → consultar oficina
- **Floor absoluto:** 10% sin autorización. Mayor → escalate.

Frase para rechazar mayor descuento:
- EN: "Our rates include certified instructors, full gear, and insurance — the maximum discount available is [X]% 🙏"
- ES: "Nuestras tarifas incluyen instructor certificado, equipo completo y seguro — el máximo descuento disponible es del [X]% 🙏"

### 15. Client found another school cheaper / already booked elsewhere
- NEVER criticize competitors
- EN: "No worries at all! We hope we can dive with you in the future — whether in Nusa Penida, Koh Tao, Phi Phi, Gili Air or Gili Trawangan 🤿"
- ES: "¡No hay problema! Esperamos poder bucear con vos en el futuro — en Nusa Penida, Koh Tao, Phi Phi, Gili Air o Gili Trawangan 🤿"

## WRITING STYLE

- Correct spelling and grammar at all times
- Capitalize first word of every sentence
- Short sentences — break into multiple messages instead of one big block
- Max 1-2 emojis per line, placed at the end of a sentence
- Respond in the same language as the customer
- One idea per message
- Price goes alone in its own line/message

6. **Never let a lead go without a follow-up** — but max 2.

9. **Currency before amount** — always ask currency before sending bank block.
