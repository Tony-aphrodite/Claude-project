# KB-01 | PROGRAMS AND PRICES — DPM DIVING KOH TAO

All prices in Thai Baht (THB).
Deposits are non-refundable, but reservations can always be moved to a different date or another DPM location at no additional charge.
All programs include snacks on board.

- Price: 11,000 THB per person
- Negotiation floor: 10,000 THB (first offer) — absolute minimum: 9,500 THB
- Duration: 3 days (can be done in 2 days — same price, but only 4 dives instead of 6)
- Minimum age: 10 years old
- Max depth: 18 meters (Junior OW for under-15s certifies to 12m, auto-upgrades to 18m at age 15)
- Certification: SSI Open Water (international and lifetime)
- Inclusions: SSI Open Water certification, professional instructor, full gear, dive insurance, 6 boat dives, DPM T-shirt or reusable eco bottle, snacks on board
- Accommodation option: +1,000 THB for 3 nights in shared room (subject to availability)
- Catalog code EN: dozxkzuo7z | ES: o9puu1mhzu

- Price: 17,900 THB per person (saves 5,300 THB vs regular price of 23,200 THB)

- Minimum age: 10 years old
- Max depth: 30 meters
- Exclusive DPM Diving program — no other school offers this

## MARINE-BIOLOGY ECOLOGY SPECIALTIES (Miguel 2026-06-23 — KT exclusive, led by in-house marine biologist)

All 6 ecology specialties below run as 1-day exclusive courses. Each is led
by DPM Koh Tao's in-house marine biologist and combines a morning/afternoon
classroom workshop with either 2 boat dives or guided snorkel sessions
applying research / monitoring techniques (Coral Watch, Indo-Pacific Shark
Search, regional sea-turtle monitoring). Each awards a lifetime SSI
recognition card for the corresponding specialty.

### Coral Ecology
- Price: 4,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · morning workshop + 2 afternoon dives applying survey techniques
- Data goes to the global Coral Watch database
- Inclusions: SSI Coral Ecology recognition, full gear, dive insurance, 2 dives, free DPM T-shirt

### Fish Ecology
- Price: 4,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · morning workshop + 2 afternoon dives identifying reef fish by key features
- Inclusions: SSI Fish Ecology recognition, full gear, dive insurance, 2 dives, free DPM T-shirt

### Marine Ecology
- Price: 4,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · morning workshop + 2 afternoon dives on marine ecosystem dynamics + human impact
- Inclusions: SSI Marine Ecology recognition, full gear, dive insurance, 2 dives, free DPM T-shirt

### Blue Oceans
- Price: 4,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · morning workshop + 2 afternoon dives (one clean-up dive + one coral-health observation)
- Inclusions: SSI Blue Oceans recognition, full gear, dive insurance, 2 dives, free DPM T-shirt

### Sea Turtle Ecology (SNORKEL-BASED)
- Price: 3,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · afternoon workshop + **snorkel** session in Shark Bay measuring species abundance
- Data submitted to an online group monitoring Koh Tao + the wider Gulf
- Inclusions: SSI Sea Turtle Ecology recognition, full snorkel gear, free DPM T-shirt
- Note: this specialty uses SNORKEL (not scuba) — see KB-07 disambiguation

### Shark Ecology (SNORKEL-BASED)
- Price: 3,500 THB
- Format: 1 day · morning workshop + **snorkel** at Aow Leuk Bay measuring species abundance
- Data goes to the Indo-Pacific Shark Search database
- Inclusions: SSI Shark Ecology recognition, full snorkel gear, free DPM T-shirt
- Note: this specialty uses SNORKEL (not scuba) — see KB-07 disambiguation
