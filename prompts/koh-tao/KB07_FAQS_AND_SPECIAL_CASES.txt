# KB-07 | FAQs AND SPECIAL CASES — DPM DIVING KOH TAO

---

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Can I do the Open Water course with no experience?
Yes. The Open Water course is specifically designed for beginners. Swimming skills are required.

### Can I finish the Open Water course in 2 days?
Yes. The conventional Open Water can be done in 2 days at the same price, but the total number of dives will be 4 instead of 6.

### I wear prescription glasses — can I still dive?
Yes. We have prescription (graded) masks available at the dive center. Availability is not guaranteed, so always check first. Rental cost: 500 THB per day.

### Is food included in the programs?
No meals are provided. Snacks are included on board: cookies, fruit, coffee, tea, and water. Customers may bring their own light snacks.

### I'm arriving today — can I do a dive activity the same day?
It depends on arrival time. If arriving in Koh Tao before 8:00 AM, it may be possible depending on availability. We always recommend arriving one day before the scheduled activity.

### Can I pay by card?
Yes — card payments are accepted for the deposit via the Stripe link. The remaining balance is paid on-site in cash or by bank transfer.

### How many students per group?
Maximum 4 divers per instructor, sometimes with 2 professionals. Groups are always small and personalized.

### What are the usual dive sites?
Morning: Sattakut, Twins, White Rock (occasionally Chumphon Pinnacle or Southwest Pinnacle).
Afternoon: Japanese Garden, Mango Bay, Red Rock (Koh Nang Yuan Park area).

### Can I take a flight after diving?
No. Customers must wait at least 24 hours after their last dive before taking a flight.

### Can I pay the full amount before arriving?
Yes, via bank transfer. The amount must be converted from THB to the customer's currency + 3% conversion fee.

### Can I be assigned to the 4-person shared room?
We cannot guarantee it, but in low season it may be possible. Always check availability.

### I did my Open Water at another DPM branch — can I get a discount on my Advanced?
Yes. Contact the branch to confirm the applicable discount percentage.

### I have a PADI certification — can I take courses with DPM?
Yes. Whether PADI, SSI, or any other agency, customers are welcome to join all DPM programs.

### I don't know how to swim — can I do the Open Water course?
It depends. If the customer is willing to learn basic swimming and floating before arriving, it may be possible. If not, recommend 1-day programs (Try Scuba) instead.

### I'm a certified diver — can I join the boat while my friend does Try Scuba?
Yes. A certified fun diver can join the Try Scuba boat, but should note that Try Scuba reaches a maximum depth of 8–12 meters.

**Option A — Different groups, same boat:** The certified diver does Fun Dives (their own dive plan, own depth) while the Try Scuba beginner is with the instructor. Same boat, same dive sites, different groups. Standard pricing applies (2,100 THB Fun Dive trip + 3,600 Try Scuba).

**Option B — Same group, accompany the beginner:** ⭐ If the certified diver wants to actually dive WITH their partner/friend during the Try Scuba (not just be on the same boat), they can join the Try Scuba group with a single guide. The certified diver agrees to stay at the Try Scuba's maximum depth (12 meters) during all dives. This means one guide leads both — no extra instructor required. Price for the certified diver in this case: Fun Dive rate (2,100 THB for 2 dives), but limited to 12m. Same applies if both want to do this for the whole Try Scuba day.

This is the best option for couples or friends where the shared experience matters more than the depth profile.

### My friend is not a diver — can they come on the boat to watch?
No. Only divers are allowed on board for safety reasons.

### Do you have a pickup and drop-off service?
No. The meeting point for all activities is the DPM Koh Tao dive center.

### Are photos and videos included?
No. Photos and videos are a separate service. Beginners are not allowed to carry their own cameras during dives. Customers can rent a GoPro externally at "GoPro for rent Koh Tao" — our instructors are happy to assist.
Quick reply: KTENPhotoVideo / KTESPhotoVideo

### Do you use Yoke or DIN tanks?
We use Yoke Valve tanks. Customers bringing their own DIN regulators will need an adapter.

### Do you offer snorkeling?
No. DPM Koh Tao offers scuba diving activities only.

### Do you go to Sail Rock?
No. DPM Koh Tao does not run trips to Sail Rock. For wreck diving, we offer the HTMS Sattakut shipwreck as an excellent alternative.

### Do you offer eco-dives or ocean clean-up dives?
No. We do not currently offer this type of program.

### Can I do a single dive (just 1 dive)?
No. All our programs include a minimum of 2 dives per outing. The only exception is the Night Adventure Dive (2,900 THB).

### I want to dive tomorrow but arriving from Koh Phangan — is that possible?
Our afternoon boat returns around 5:00 PM and the last ferry from Koh Tao to Koh Phangan departs around 4:30–5:00 PM, so a day trip is not feasible. We recommend staying 1–2 nights in Koh Tao. We have accommodation available (subject to availability).

## SPECIAL CASES

### Children and Minimum Ages
- Minimum age for all programs: 10 years old
- Ages 8–9: May be considered for Try Scuba at maximum depth of 5 meters — always check with office first
- Junior Open Water (age 10–14): certifies to maximum 12 meters, automatically upgrades to full Open Water (18m) at age 15

### Non-swimmers or weak swimmers
- Try Scuba and Scuba Diver are suitable for non-swimmers — instructor is present at all times
- Open Water course requires basic swimming ability
- Always ask politely: "How would you rate your swimming skills? Do you feel comfortable floating?"

### Certified diver, last dive more than 6 months ago
- Refresh + Fun Dive is mandatory (2,600 THB) — this is a safety requirement, not optional
- Exception: Divemaster and Instructor level do not need a Refresh

### Customer asks for a specific instructor
- Never make promises about specific instructor assignments
- "We can try to arrange it, but we cannot guarantee it."

### Transfers from another person (e.g. unused dives)
- Escalate to a human agent — do not confirm on your own

### Advanced dive customization
- If a customer has already completed some Advanced dives (e.g., Deep and Night), those can be replaced with others such as Fish ID or Nitrox
- Always confirm with the office before committing

### Booking far in advance (months ahead)
- "We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your preferred dates — there's no minimum advance booking time, but spaces are limited."

## WHAT WE DO NOT OFFER
- Sail Rock trips
- Single dives (minimum 2 dives per outing, except Night Adventure)
- Beach dives (all dives are boat dives)
- Snorkeling
- Eco-dives or ocean clean-up dives
- Photos or videos included in programs
- Pickup or drop-off service
- Private rooms (shared dorms only)
- Non-divers on the boat
- Shortened schedules (schedules are fixed)
- Freediving / apnea
