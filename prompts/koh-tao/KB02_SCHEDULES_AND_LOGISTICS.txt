# KB-02 | SCHEDULES AND LOGISTICS — DPM DIVING KOH TAO

---

## CRITICAL: ALL PROGRAMS USE BOAT SPACE

**There is NO pool at DPM Koh Tao.** All confined water sessions (theory + skills practice) are done in a shallow bay reached by boat. This means:
- Every program requires boat space — always check roster availability
- NEVER say "pool session" or "piscina"
- ALWAYS say "shallow water session in the bay" / "sesión en aguas poco profundas en la bahía"
- Spanish-speaking clients start 1 hour later: 9:30am instead of 8:30am

---

## SCHEDULE BY PROGRAM

### Try Scuba / Bautizo de Buceo
- Meet at DPM: 11:15am (PM boat)
- Return: ~16:45pm
- Structure: theory class → boat to shallow bay → 2 dives (max 12m)
- Full day program

### Scuba Diver
- Meet at DPM: 11:15am (PM boat)
- Return: ~16:45pm
- Structure: theory → skills in shallow bay → 2 boat dives

### Open Water Course (3 days) — STANDARD SCHEDULE
**Day 1:**
- Meet at DPM: 11:15am (PM boat)
- Theory session + confined water skills in shallow bay
- Return: ~16:45pm

**Day 2:**
- Meet at DPM: 5:45am (AM boat) or 11:15am (PM boat) — depends on roster
- 2 open water dives
- Return: ~10am or ~16:45pm

**Day 3:**
- Schedule decided during Day 2 based on group progress
- Option A: AM boat — meet 5:45am, return ~10am
- Option B: PM boat — meet 11:15am, return ~16:45pm
- Includes knowledge review and final dives

*Spanish-speaking clients: typically start Day 1 at PM boat (11:15am)*

**2-Day option (same price, 4 dives instead of 6):**
Available on request. Same boat times apply.

### Open Water 30 (3 days)
Same schedule as Open Water Course above.
Max depth: 30 meters (vs 18m for conventional OW)

### Advanced Course (2 days)
**Day 1:** 
- Check roster for AM or PM slot
- 2 dives: Deep Dive + Navigation (or other specialties)

**Day 2:**
- 2-3 dives including Night Dive (typically evening/night)
- 1 dive: Wreck Dive

*Duration: 2 consecutive days — check availability for BOTH days before confirming*

### Fun Dives
- **AM boat:** meet DPM 5:45am, return ~10:00am (2 dives)
- **PM boat:** meet DPM 11:15am, return ~16:45pm (2 dives)
- **Night dive:** meet DPM 17:45pm, return ~19:45pm (1 dive — OW cert minimum)
- Both AM and PM trips visit 2 different dive sites

### Refresh + Fun Dive
- Same schedule as Fun Dives
- Includes skills review in shallow bay before open water dive

### Night Adventure Dive
- Meet at DPM: 17:45pm
- Return: ~19:45pm
- Duration: approximately 2 hours
- Requirement: Open Water certification minimum

### Deep Adventure + Fun Dive
- Same boat as Fun Dives
- Morning or afternoon option
- 2 dives: 1 deep dive (30m) + 1 fun dive

### Rescue Course (3 days)
- Consult roster for availability
- Mixes skills practice in shallow bay + open water rescue scenarios
- Requires Advanced certification

## LOGISTICS

### Pre-course (day before starting):
- Client must visit DPM shop the day before to register and verify gear sizes
- Office hours: 8:00am – 20:00pm (8pm)
- Location: see KTLOCATION snippet

### On arrival day:
- Registration + gear fitting
- Team will introduce themselves and explain next day's schedule

### What to bring:
- Swimwear
- Sunscreen (reef-safe preferred)
- Towel
- Any medication if needed
- Passport or ID (for certification)

### What's included in all programs:
- Full dive gear (wetsuit, BCD, regulator, tank, fins, mask)
- Professional instructor or dive guide
- SSI dive insurance (certification courses only — NOT included in Fun Dives, Deep Adventure + FD or Refresh; see SEGURO rule in system prompt)
- Snacks on board
- Boat transfers to all dive sites

### Today availability logic (Koh Tao = GMT+7):
- Client arriving before 10am → PM boat still possible (check roster)
- Client arriving after 11am → recommend tomorrow AM boat
- Client arriving after 4pm → only night boat possible (cert OW+ required), otherwise tomorrow

NEVER confirm a slot without checking the roster first.

### Client asking about "today availability":
→ Always verify roster before confirming. Never say "yes we have space" without checking.
→ If PM still open: "We do have afternoon availability today — want me to lock that in? 🤿"
→ If full: "Today is fully booked but we have space tomorrow morning — shall I hold that for you? 🤿"
